---
title: How do I connect Discord to OlympiTech?
description: Connect your Discord server for seamless integration.
---

## Quick Steps

1. Go to your OlympiTech dashboard
2. Click "Integrations" > "Discord"
3. Select "Connect Discord Server"
4. Authorize the bot in your Discord server
5. Server syncs instantly

## Notes

- You must have "Manage Server" permission in Discord
- Once connected, plugin activity appears in your Discord channels
- Reconnect anytime via the dashboard

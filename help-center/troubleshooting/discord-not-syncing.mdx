---
title: Discord Roles Not Syncing
description: Quick fix for role sync issues between OlympiTech and Discord.
---

## Issue

- Discord roles do not update after plugin changes
- No sync messages in Discord

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Re-authorize Bot">
    ````
    Remove OlympiTech bot from your Discord, then reconnect via the Integrations page.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Permissions">
    ````
    Ensure the bot has permission to manage roles in your server settings.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Force Sync">
    ````
    Click "Force Sync" next to Discord integration in your OlympiTech dashboard.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>

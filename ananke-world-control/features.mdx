Stop your world from getting messy or laggy.

### What is it?
A set of simple switches to turn off annoying Minecraft mechanics.

### Capabilities
- **Fire Spread**: Stop fire from burning down forests or houses.
- **Weather**: Keep it sunny forever. No rain or thunder.
- **Decay**: Stop leaves from disappearing when you cut down trees.
- **Ice/Snow**: Stop snow from piling up or ice from forming on water.

## ğŸš« Block & Item Restrictions

Ban specific items or blocks.

### What is it?
If you don't want players to have TNT, or you don't want them to place Bedrock, you can block it.

### How it works
- **Placing**: You can ban placing specific blocks (like TNT).
- **Crafting**: You can ban crafting specific items.
- **Usage**: You can stop players from using Flint and Steel or Buckets.
